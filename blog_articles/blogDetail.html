    Action's popular kamado BBQ is much cheaper than
    the expensive brand version

    Emma de Witte • SALARY & Wealth • 23 July 2025, 14:32
    SALARY & Wealth
    Ad Space

    Even though the Dutch sun is hiding behind the clouds, this is the best time to buy a good barbecue. Action has a popular kamado barbecue, which looks a lot like a Big Green Egg, and is now at a reduced price.

    Ad Space

    Before the summer, Action's kamado barbecue was already sharply priced at €499, but now the discount chain has topped that off with another discount. Anyone who wants to be the grillmaster on those last summer evenings can buy a brand-new barbecue for the reasonable price of €444.

    Popular Action kamado barbecue
    discounted

    Since summer isn't over yet, Action will have replenished its stock of kamado barbecues. Moreover, this model is popular among grill enthusiasts. It offers an affordable alternative to the respected Big Green Eggs, which are made of the same material.

    Both models share the same look and are made of ceramic, ensuring even heat distribution within the basket. The Action kamado barbecue is currently discounted by a few tens of euros, making it one of the most affordable kamados on the market.

    The popular Big Green Egg easily costs over €2000. That's a difference of almost 80%. This barbecue also features cast iron parts and wooden accents, and in addition to grilling, it also offers slow cooking, where food is cooked slowly.

    This allows you to prepare delicious barbecue dishes, such as a tender steak, pulled pork, or even a crispy pizza. And of course, you can also grill fish and vegetables on the large surface of the 21-inch kamado, should any of your guests wish to do so.

    Don't have a large outdoor space, but still love barbecuing? Then Action's compact table barbecue might be a good alternative!

    ACTION - €444
    Ad Space

    Includes thermometer and can be ordered online

    Whether you're an experienced grillmaster or a beginner, the Action kamado barbecue suits all skill levels. The included thermometer makes grilling even easier. This allows you to maintain control and prevent the meat from burning.

    It also has handy side tables for preparing meat, fish, or vegetable skewers. It also features a wheeled base, making it easy to move the barbecue to the shed or storage room once everyone has finished eating.

    Another practical feature is the opening under the grill . This makes it easy to refill charcoal and store it away afterward. If you buy this affordable Action kamado barbecue now, you'll have plenty of summer evenings to enjoy grilling with friends and family.

    Perhaps the best part is that you can leave the car in the driveway. No need to drive to an Action store; you can simply order the barbecue online from the webshop. For €29.99 in shipping costs, the entire package will be delivered to your home within 5 business days.
